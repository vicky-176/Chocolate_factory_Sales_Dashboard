# Chocolate Factory Sales Dashboard

This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that analyzes chocolate factory sales performance across **countries, products, and time periods**. The dashboard focuses on understanding **shipment volume and revenue trends** to support business decisions.

## Repository Contents
- `Chocolate_Factory_Sales_Dashboard.pbix` — Power BI dashboard file  
- `Chocolate_Factory_Sales.csv` — Source dataset

## Tech Stack
**Tool:** Microsoft Power BI  
**Data Source:** CSV  
**Modeling:** Power Query, DAX

## Dashboard Overview

### 1. Boxes Shipped by Country
A bar chart showing the **total number of boxes shipped** across:
- Australia
- Canada
- UK
- India
- USA
- New Zealand  
This highlights which regions contribute the most to overall distribution.

### 2. Sales Amount Over Time
A line chart showing the **sum of sales amount by month (2022)**.  
This helps identify **growth, decline, and seasonal patterns** in revenue.

### 3. Boxes Shipped by Product
A horizontal bar chart ranking products such as:
- 50% Dark Bites
- Smooth Silky Salty
- Eclairs
- Caramel Stuffed Bars
- Milk Bars, White Choc, and others  
This reveals **top-selling and low-performing products** by shipment volume.

### 4. Product Contribution to Sales Change
A waterfall chart showing how different products **increase or decrease total sales amount** between time periods.  
This helps identify **which products drive growth and which reduce overall revenue**.

## Business Insight
The dashboard enables quick identification of:
- **High-performing countries** by shipment volume
- **Top chocolate products** driving distribution
- **Monthly revenue trends**
- **Product-level impact on sales changes**
